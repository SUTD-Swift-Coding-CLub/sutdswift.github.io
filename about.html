About Us

The first of its kind, SUTD Swift Coding Club prides itself as the joint collaboration between SUTD and the Apple Education programme, and is the sole pioneer of Swift Education at the tertiary-education level. Club members are all proud members of the iOS programming community.

Mission Statement

To build a collaborative and innovative community of Swift learners and kickstart the journey to become a proficient iOS developer.

About Us

The first of its kind, SUTD Swift Coding Club prides itself as the joint collaboration between SUTD and the Apple Education programme, and is the sole pioneer of Swift Education at the tertiary-education level. All club members are proud members of the iOS programming community.

Mission Statement

To build a collaborative and innovative community of Swift learners and kickstart the journey to become a proficient iOS developer.

Club Values

Activeness, Fun, Vibrancy

To build a collaborative and innovative community of Swift learners and kickstart the journey to become a proficient iOS developer.

Contribution to SUTD & Community

Creativity & Innovation

Members are freely able to pursue whatever project they wish, and can conduct any Swift-related workshops. Creativity and innovation is not only in abundance in our club, but is the core value here.

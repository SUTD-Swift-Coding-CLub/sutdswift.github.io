Inclusive App Dev Hackathon 2022

In our very first inaugural hackathon, teams of 3 to 4 students will design and code apps in Swift to tackle challenges provided by a non-profit organisation, the Singapore Association of the Visually Handicapped (SAVH). The aim of this hackathon is to support the students throughout the complete app development process - from designing a relevant solution, to developing a functional app and ultimately, publishing their applications to the App Store for everyone to use.

Design Phase

01. Swift IAP Workshop

This workshop provides teams with training on how to design and build inclusive iOS apps with Apple-certified training materials. It is open to non-participants of the hackathon too! We have completed this workshop and students are now prepared to showcase their ideas.

02. Swift Design Showcase

Happening on 22 January 2022 from 1 to 3 PM, teams will present their inclusive app solutions through a 3-minute presentation and receive feedback from guest speakers from SUTD and SAVH. We will also be showcasing innovative iOS app designs by upcoming young secondary school coders.
